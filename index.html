    The Blockchain Hive

    Come to learn, stay to learn

    The Blockchain Hive (Deducation Academy) is an initiative by IIT Bombay students that seeks to revolutionize education by creating a decentralized platform that incentivizes people to learn in innovative ways. In addition to its educational platform, The Blockchain Hive is also working to raise awareness about Blockchain and Web3 through a range of educational courses and offline events. These initiatives aim to equip learners with the knowledge and skills needed to participate in the decentralized world and to help them navigate the fast-evolving landscape of blockchain technology
